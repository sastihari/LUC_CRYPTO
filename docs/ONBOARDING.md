## Step 1: Set Up a Wallet 🔐

### Option A: MetaMask (Recommended)

**On Desktop:**
1. Go to https://metamask.io
2. Click "Download"
3. Add extension to your browser (Chrome, Firefox, Brave)
4. Create new wallet
5. **WRITE DOWN YOUR SEED PHRASE ON PAPER!**
6. Confirm seed phrase
7. Set a strong password

**On Mobile:**
1. Download MetaMask from App Store / Play Store
2. Create new wallet
3. Follow same steps as desktop

### Option B: Other Wallets
- Trust Wallet
- Coinbase Wallet
- Rainbow Wallet

---

## Step 2: Add Polygon Network 🌐

LUC runs on Polygon, so you need to add this network to your wallet.

### Automatic (Easy):
1. Visit https://chainlist.org
2. Search "Polygon"
3. Click "Add to MetaMask"
4. Approve in your wallet

### Manual (If automatic fails):

**Network Details:**
```
Network Name: Polygon Mainnet
RPC URL: https://polygon-rpc.com
Chain ID: 137
Currency Symbol: MATIC
Block Explorer: https://polygonscan.com
```

**To add manually:**
1. Open MetaMask
2. Click network dropdown (top)
3. Click "Add Network"
4. Enter details above
5. Save

---

## Step 3: Get Some MATIC ⛽

You need MATIC (Polygon's native token) to pay for transaction fees.

### Option A: Buy on Exchange
1. Create account on Binance, Coinbase, or Kraken
2. Buy MATIC
3. Withdraw to your Polygon wallet address

### Option B: Bridge from Ethereum
1. Go to https://wallet.polygon.technology
2. Connect your wallet
3. Bridge ETH or other tokens to Polygon

### How much do you need?
- **Minimum:** $5-10 worth of MATIC
- **Recommended:** $20-50 for multiple transactions
- Gas fees on Polygon are very cheap (~$0.01 per transaction)

---

## Step 4: Add LUC Token 🔥

Once you have MATIC, add LUC to your wallet:

1. Open MetaMask
2. Click "Import tokens"
3. Enter:
   - Contract Address: `[CONTRACT_ADDRESS]`
   - Symbol: LUC
   - Decimals: 18
4. Click "Add Custom Token"
5. LUC now appears in your wallet!

⚠️ **SECURITY TIP:** Always verify the contract address on PolygonScan!

---

## Step 5: Buy Some LUC 💎

### On QuickSwap (Recommended):

1. Go to https://quickswap.exchange
2. Click "Connect Wallet"
3. Select MetaMask
4. In the swap interface:
   - From: MATIC (or USDC)
   - To: LUC (paste contract address)
5. Enter amount to swap
6. Click "Swap"
7. Confirm in MetaMask
8. Done! LUC is now in your wallet

### Tips for Buying:
- Start with a small amount to test
- Set slippage to 0.5-1%
- Check the price impact
- Never rush large purchases

## 🛡️ Security Best Practices

### DO:
✅ Write down seed phrase on paper
✅ Store paper in safe place
✅ Double-check addresses before sending
✅ Start with small amounts
✅ Use hardware wallet for large holdings
✅ Verify contract addresses on PolygonScan

### DON'T:
❌ Share your seed phrase with ANYONE
❌ Store seed phrase digitally (no photos, no cloud)
❌ Click suspicious links
❌ Connect wallet to unknown sites
❌ Rush transactions
❌ Invest more than you can afford to lose

## ⚠️ Common Scams to Avoid

### 1. Fake Support
- Team will NEVER DM you first
- We will NEVER ask for your seed phrase
- Always verify official channels

### 2. Fake Tokens
- Always verify contract address
- Check on PolygonScan
- Don't trust random airdrops

### 3. Phishing Sites
- Bookmark official sites
- Check URLs carefully
- Don't click links in emails/DMs

### 4. Pump and Dump Groups
- Avoid "guaranteed profit" groups
- No one can guarantee returns
- If it sounds too good to be true, it is
